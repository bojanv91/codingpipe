## About

Hello World! I'm Bojan Veljanovski, a software engineer and technology manager with 15+ years experience architecting and building software, turning product needs into scalable technical solutions, and leading teams.

I started programming in 2003 with VB6 apps as a teenager. Since then I've designed and built web applications, mobile apps, and enterprise systems across travel, hospitality, property management, security & access control, and smart wearables. I've led development teams, been a CTO, and mostly worked with startups and SMEs - bridging product vision and technical implementation.

This blog is where I document what I find interesting to keep as a reference.
